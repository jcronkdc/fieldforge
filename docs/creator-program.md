# Founding Creator Program

## Purpose
Recruit an initial cohort of storytellers and world-builders who will seed MythaTron with premium content, stress-test tools, and evangelize the platform.

## Target Profile
- Experienced fanfic / serial fiction writers interested in collaborative worlds.
- Narrative designers from indie games, ARGs, or tabletop communities.
- AI-curious creators experimenting with LLM-assisted storytelling.

## Value Proposition
- Early access to MythaTron’s authoring suite (swipe feed, remix analytics, AI co-authoring).
- Revenue share on premium branches once monetization launches.
- Spotlight placement in the swipe feed, LangBot prompts, and marketing site.
- Direct influence on product roadmap via weekly creator councils.

## Outreach Materials
- **Landing Page Copy (draft):**
  > *Become a Founding World Builder.* Shape the mythos of MythaTron, co-write with AI co-authors, and lead a universe of collaborators from day one.

- **Email Template:**
  ```text
  Subject: Help us launch the next storytelling universe

  Hi {Name},

  We’re building MythaTron—an AI-native storytelling platform where worlds evolve with every swipe, remix, and branch. We love how you {reference specific work} and would be honored to have you as a Founding World Builder.

  Founding creators receive:
  • Early access to collaborative story tools and analytics
  • Revenue share on premium branches when we launch monetization
  • Feature placement in the MythaTron swipe feed and cross-platform bots
  • Weekly sessions with our product team to shape the roadmap

  If you’re interested, reply and we’ll schedule a 20-minute kickoff. We’re onboarding a limited cohort this month.

  – MythaTron Team
  ```

- **One-Pager Outline:**
  - Vision overview (AI-native storytelling universe).
  - Features snapshot (Remix flow, story genome analytics, cross-platform syndication).
  - Creator benefits (monetization, spotlight, community).
  - Timeline (Phase 1 MVP dates, upcoming events).

## Onboarding Flow
1. Discovery call (20 min) covering creator goals and world ideas.
2. Grant dashboard access + private Discord channel.
3. Provide starter kit:
   - Story world template doc (lore bible, character sheets).
   - Tutorial videos on remix flow and analytics dashboard.
   - Calendar of weekly creator council meetings.
4. Co-develop launch plan for their first story arc (goal: 3 branches in first week, 10 remixes in first month).

## Weekly Rituals
- **Creator Council (1 hour):** share metrics, upcoming features, pain points.
- **Story Spotlight:** rotate a world at the top of swipe feed + LangBot prompts.
- **Growth Clinic:** workshop social amplification strategies and A/B test new prompt formats.

## Incentives & Rewards
- Launch bonus: $100 credit or gift card for creators who ship 3 branches in first week.
- Referral bonus: invite another creator, earn 5% of their first month’s premium revenue.
- Achievement badges (e.g., “Universe Architect” for 100 remixes).

## Tracking & KPIs
- Number of founding creators active weekly.
- Average branches produced per creator.
- Remix volume per founding world.
- Retention D1/D7/D30 of founding creators.
- Satisfaction surveys (monthly) / NPS.
